    • THE ARTIST

    Hello, and thank you for visiting my website! My name is April. I am the wife of an awesome husband and mother of three wonderful grown children who inspire me to create fun new beaded flowers. I started beading in 2003, but have been an artist since childhood. My love of gardening and creating art led me to teach myself, with the help of a few books, to bead flowers using wire and beads. I am always amazed at how the huge variety of bead colors available can work together to make unique, beautiful pieces of art.

    Aside from beading flowers, I love nature, animals, gardening, and most of all, family. I was diagnosed with breast cancer in 2016, and eventually beat it with the love and support of my family. I now spend my time making personalized crafts that speak to people's emotions.
